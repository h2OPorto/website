About the Area

Located near the Douro River, our apartments offer easy access to the heart of Porto. Supermarkets, bakeries, and iconic landmarks are all within walking distance.

  • Closest supermarket: Pingo Doce – 300m
  • Must-see: Palácio da Bolsa, Ribeira, Dom Luís I Bridge
  • Local tip: Visit Jardim do Morro for sunset views!
